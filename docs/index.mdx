# What is Mimid?

Mimid is a music player designed for learning songs by ear.

Mimid runs in your browser and is designed for your phone.
Mimid is free to use, ad-free, and free and open source.

## Features

- Large, easy-to-press buttons.
- Change playback speed with a single tap.
- Loop difficult sections of a song.
- Precise controls for jumping forward and backward in a song.
- Highly customizable.
- No MP3 files needed.

## Three principles

Mimid was built with three principles in mind:

1. Usability
2. Simplicity
3. Customizability
