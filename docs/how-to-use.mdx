## Video URL Bar

_Text box that takes in the YouTube Video URL of the song you want to learn._

Here's how you load a song in Mimid:

1. Open YouTube and look for the song you want to learn.
2. Copy the video URL of the song.
3. Open Mimid and paste the video URL into the Video URL Bar.

## YouTube video player

_The embedded YouTube video player that plays the song._

## Play Button

_Button used to play and pause the song._

## Speed Button

_Button used to slow down the playback speed._

This is a toggle button.
Press it once to slow down the song.
Press it again to go back to normal speed.

## Loop Button

_Button used to loop a part of the song._

Here's how you use this button to loop a part of the song you're playing:

1. Press the button once to mark the start of the loop.
2. Press it again to mark the end of the loop.
3. The song will start looping.
4. Press the button again to clear the loop.

Here's what the letters on the button mean:

- **A:** Mark the start of the loop.
- **B:** Mark the end of the loop.
- **X:** Clear the loop.

## Skip buttons

_Buttons used to skip backward and forward in the song._
